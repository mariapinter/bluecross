About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

This is not your typical medical practice. We are very different than traditional medical practices. Everything your doctor does is specifically tailored for your benefit. Your doctor is always attentive, responsive and available when you need her/him. That's what healthcare is all about.

When you call, a highly trained member of our staff will attend to your needs immediately. When you visit, your appointments with your doctor will be punctual and unhurried. If you need your doctor after-hours, you can reach her/him on his cell phone or through our answering service 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Healthcare for Kids

You can simply book an appointment and take your European Health Insurance Card (EHIC) and your child with you.

Medical Consulting

Our experienced and highly effective team of consultants and doctors work according to the latest scientific and methodological findings and standards.

Modern Equipments
